Research Area

Neutrino physics, early Universe processes

Research Interests

I am interested in the origin of fermion masses and flavour mixing and the role of neutrinos in cosmology. Currently, I am considering the following aspects: new approaches for realizing flavor mixing, lepto-genesis and DM-genesis via cosmological phase transition, photon circular polarisation in astro-particle processes and direct and indirect measurements of discrete flavor symmetries.
